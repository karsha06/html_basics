vroom kenya is a car dealership incorporated in Kenya dealing with importation and sale of new and second-hand Motor Vehicles. Vehicles in our dealership are sourced from Japan, Thailand, United Kingdom, Australia, South Africa and host of other countries to ensure variety of units to meet market demand
